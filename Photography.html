Photography

One of my favorite practices is analog photography. Without the possibility of instant review or retakes, each image captures a moment as it unfolds, spontaneous, unposed, and authentic. I’m drawn to the unpredictability of film and the way it preserves a sense of presence and intimacy. I primarily shoot on 35mm film, embracing grain and imperfections as part of the narrative. Through my work, I aim to document fleeting, everyday moments that might otherwise go unnoticed. I believe it is important to preserve and continue the culture of film photography in an increasingly digital world. I shoot in 35mm with mostly PORTRA 4OO film, I like to shoot portraits and objects.
